KAIS 2020 - Files

These are the files submitted as part of the KAIS submission. The authors realize the best practices and guidelines in publishing ontologies, but wish to publish the ontologies after peer-review. The following files are provided:
  1. data.sql - the synthetic dataset as a MySQL dump
  2. mapping.ttl - the R2RML mapping from the database (2) to the ontology (1)
  3. data-as-rdf-after-mapping.ttl - result of executing (3)
