I finally got around to reading the third novel in the [The Long Earth](https://thelongearth.fandom.com/wiki/The_long_Earth_Wiki) series, namely [The Long Mars](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/18586487-the-long-mars), written by [Sir Terry Pratchett](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Terry_Pratchett) and [Stephen Baxter](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stephen_Baxter_(author)).  It took me years to read this book, mostly because it was promised as a gift that finally arrived after ages.

While it took me a little while to get into the book, I gather that was because it took so long! I read the second installment in 2019, so a long while ago. Once I got into the story, it was a page-turner. I loved reading this book.

My rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
