[Lettre aux ingénieurs qui doutent](https://www.lechappee.org/collections/pour-en-finir-avec/lettre-aux-ingenieurs-qui-doutent) by [Olivier Lefebvre](https://www.lechappee.org/auteurs/olivier-lefebvre) was an easy and enjoyable read. The book also made me think about the role of engineers in this society and how some feel a disconnect between their job as engineers and their core values. This book really got me thinking about the (mis)alignment between my job and my values.

My rating: ⭐⭐⭐

You may be wondering why it took so long to finish this book? Well, I'm reading a PhD thesis and I'm also reviewing some articles in my spare time!
