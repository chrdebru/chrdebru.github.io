I attended a lovely gala evening organized by my significant other and hosted by [Edna Sorgelson](https://rupaulsdragrace.fandom.com/wiki/Edna_Sorgelsen), a player at one of my D&D tables. The charity evening was organized to collect funds for the [Le Refuge LGBTQIA+](https://www.lrlo.be/). The evening was amazing, and the artists were fantastic. There was even a show by the [Q-Team](https://theqteam.be/), a queer geek choir in Brussels, whose leader also sits in one of my D&D tables. 

My rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
