The second volume of this epic journey. One of the things I enjoyed most about [The Two Towers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Two_Towers) is how the pace became much more consistent throughout. The story flows naturally from one scene to the next.

Technically, the book we know as "The Two Towers" is itself two books (Books III and IV) bound together. So I'm counting this one as two books.

My rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
