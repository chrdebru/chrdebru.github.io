I was asked to read [Ce monstre… qui m'a dévoré](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/243381209-ce-monstre-qui-m-a-d-vor) by [Federico Ariu](https://www.federicoariu.be/), and I was not disappointed. It is a compelling autobiographic retelling of the author's experience with a narcissistic and abusive boyfriend and drugs. The book was written in a very accessible way, which, in a weird way, made the text more intimate and personal.

My rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
