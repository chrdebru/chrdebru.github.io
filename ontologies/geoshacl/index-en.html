intuitive equals constraintni back to ToC or Named Individual ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/geoshacl#intuitiveEqualsConstraint

An alternative implementation of the "equals" relationship between two geometries. The Simple Features Access and GeoSPARQL assume that two points can never be equivalent as their boundaries are empty and geometries must share the same non-empty boundary. This can be counter-intuitive. This implementation of "equals" is based on GeoSPARQL's sf:within and sf:contains filter functions.
belongs to
constraint component c
